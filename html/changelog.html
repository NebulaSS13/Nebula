27 January 2021

MistakeNot4892 updated:

  • Will the bot generate the changelog now? Let's find out.
  • Coat racks are now material-based and craftable.
  • Coat rack backend has been rewritten to support hanging any hat or suit item.
  • A bunch of structures have been rewritten to use some shared code. As a result, you can examine most structures to see some tips on how to interact with them, and you will find several construction/deconstruction steps have changed.
  • Metal structures must be dismantled with a welding tool, while other materials use a crowbar.
  • Girders are now 'supports', and are reinforced by using a material stack on them before anchoring. Using a screwdriver will remove the reinforcement, or toggle whether or not the support will produce a fake wall.
  • This is going to be buggy, please report issues and problems.

N8-Toe updated:

  • Added chemical saftey suits to code, protect against gas in the air but need to be used with a face covering mask, unimplemented at this time.

SierraKomodo updated:

  • Disposal bin stuffing and flushing are now attack logs instead of admin logs.
  • Cutting the ID Scan wire on doors will now disable ID scanning. If the door is 'secure', this blocks access entirely. For all other doors, this fully bypasses access requirements.
  • Windoors are now properly emag-able.
  • Emagged windoors will now provide feedback that they are broken when you try to close them.
  • You can now tell if a door's control board has been fried by examining the door with an IT skill of trained or higher.
  • 'Mild' labels in medical scans are now highlighted in yellow and bold to make them more noticeable.
  • Windows and airlock windows are now paintable.
  • Smothering people with a rag and hitting people with an ignited rag now generates attack logs.
  • You now require a grab to smother people with a rag.
  • Smothering people with a rag now has a timed progress bar - 3 seconds if your CQC skill is trained or above, 6 seconds for everyone else.
  • General station/ship lights now have randomized tones - The old default warm yellow, a cool blue, and pure white.

SierrqKomodo updated:

  • Banned player's mobs now have their ckey stripped so they display the permanent SSD message and instantly cryo.

SolatK updated:

  • Dropped mobs should not take the same direction with those who held them
  • CE rig now have a boots

SomeoneStoleMyNickname updated:

  • Added a new digital pencode tag. [redacted] creates the string R E D A C E D in black lettes on black background to give the illusion of redacted contend in reports.

The Stalker updated:

  • Finished up some unused void suits. So they have stats (finally)
  • Added new materials to vender and recipes. Will be doing a part two i gues. Will eventually look into finishing the cotton plant or make a mega edition pack of new weapons

Winter updated:

  • Added textbooks. Textbooks are items that give skillbuffs while you are actively using them. Textbooks require basic literacy to use, and you must have the specific textbook for your skill level.
  • Added a book merchant who sells textbooks.
  • Molluscs and Mollusc meat now have monetary value.
  • Tradeship now always starts the round with a book trader available.
  • Tradeship has four randomized textbooks in various locations at roundstart.
  • Please report balance issues (and of course bugs) that may arrise from this feature.
  • Bonus: Codex links now open new windows.
  • Those with Master Literacy can now make their own textbooks. Blank textbooks are acquired via an autobinder and then written using a pen.
  • Includes codex information on this process.
  • Made textbooks in general to be one size smaller to allow being put in bags.
  • Very basic support for fabricators to get colours from something else other than pipe_colors
  • Unsure how this impacts skill balance, please report on that if there are issues.

comma updated:

  • Desert planets now have 'dry mud' turfs in the areas where quicksands can spawn.
  • Added new type of toolbox - electrician's. It holds all needed tools for machinery components interactions and several boxes of most common components. Can be found in supply (electrical maintenance pack) and electrical locker
  • Engineering vendomats now vend APC/air alarm kits instead of just circuitboards.
  • Most machinery components are now tiny rather than small
  • APCs can be fixed again after explosions
  • APCs now don't draw screen overlay / glow if screen component is destroyed
  • APCs now don't report cell power in UI etc if battery backup is destroyed
  • APCs now show that external power is not coming in if terminal component is destroyed
  • Machines can actually be destroyed fully by strong explosions again (not just component damage)
  • Added categories to textile designs
  • Costs for armor items are adjusted, generally more expensive to produce
  • Holsters now leave their fibers on things put inside.
  • Crowbarring doors now leaves scuffing on them, visible when examined up close. Can be repaired as any other damage.
  • Gas tanks will now dump their content into air when destroyed

eckff updated:

  • Backported Adherent species from Baystation12.
  • Backported Lizards species (previously Unathi).
  • Lizard people get new dark sprite.
  • Fixes monitor mask initial world icon.
  • Adds radial icon choice for monitors.
  • Roundstart can now be any hour.
